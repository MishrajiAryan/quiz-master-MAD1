Quiz Instructions

1. Examination Timer

The clock will be set at the server. The countdown timer at the top right corner of the screen will display the remaining time available for you to complete the examination. When the timer reaches zero, the examination will end automatically. You do not need to manually submit your paper. You can use the submit button given below if you want to end it sooner.

2. Answering Questions

Each question will be displayed along with its options. Read each question carefully before attempting it.

3. Multiple Choice Questions (MCQ)

For MCQ type questions:

  • Choose one answer from the 4 options (1, 2, 3, 4) given below the question by clicking on the radio button (circle) placed before the chosen option.
  • To change your chosen answer, click on the radio button of another option.
4. Technical Issues

In case of any technical issues (e.g., internet connectivity problems, power outage), please contact the admin or support team. Your time will be compensated as deemed appropriate by the examination committee.

5. Code of Conduct

You are expected to maintain the highest standards of academic integrity during the examination. Any form of cheating, plagiarism, or use of unfair means will result in immediate disqualification.

You must agree before beginning the quiz.
